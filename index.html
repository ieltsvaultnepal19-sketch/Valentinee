Valentine #1


And it already feels unforgettable.

This is our first Valentine together.

Your first.

Mine too.


And I love that we get to experience this

for the very first time —

with each other.

Not flowers.

Not chocolates.

Not the date.


What makes today special

is that it marks the beginning

of something that will repeat

for the rest of our lives.

Imagine us years from now…


Valentine #10.

Valentine #25.

Valentine #50.


Still choosing each other.

Still celebrating.

Still smiling the same way.

There are more than 70 Valentines waiting for us.


And I don’t just want to count them.

I want to build them.

Grow through them.

Laugh through them.

Win life together through them.

The best part?


This is only Valentine #1.


And I already know —

the story only gets better from here. ❤️
